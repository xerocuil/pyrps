{% block title %}How to Play{% endblock %}

Overview

2+ Players / Turn-Based / 4+ Six-sided Dice

  • Rank is assigned to the Pilot and is independent of Mechs.
  • Each Pilot is awarded 1 Rank Point per battle completed. The winner gains an additional Rank Point.
  • Before battle, additional Rank Points may be distributed as additional Stat, Provision, or HP (1 RP = 100 HP) bonuses.
  • For new Mechs, total player Rank Points can be distributed as modifiers.
  • Base class stats: No. of dice rolled for action.
  • Modifier: amount added to roll.

On Each Turn

Player chooses action at start of turn, Attack: Skill, or Energy Potion. Turn ends after action.

Attacks

  • Pilot rolls 1d6 per Stat
    • If opposing mech has specialty in Saving Throw stat, opposing pilot is allowed a 1d6 Saving Throw.
    • Saving Throw is successful if Roll ≥5.
  • Special Attacks allow an additional 1d6 and consume one skill point.
  • Normal Attacks consume one (1) Shield Point, Special Attacks consume two (2) Shield Points.
  • Heavy Attacks that deal Critical damage can disarm opponent and/or remove armor shards from Mech:
    • No. of armor shards removed = Strength Stat.
    • Each armor shard removed = -20 HP
  • If opponent is shielded (≥1 Shield Point), attack damage is reduced by 50%.
  • Attack damage = (Roll + Modifier) x 10
  • Critical damage = Attack Damage ≥ Stat x 5
  • Attacking ends the Pilot’s turn.
Attack Type Stat Saving Throw
Melee STR Dodge (DEX), Block (DEF)
Heavy DEF Counter (STR), Evade (DEX)
Ranged DEX Shield (DEF), Deflect (STR)

Skills

  • Consumes one Skill Point. Only one Skill Point can be spent per turn.
    • Special Attack: Allows for an additional 1d6 on attack, may include additional status effects. Ends Pilot’s turn.
    • Perk: Any effects are applied immediately, player may resume turn.

Energy Potions

Player chooses potion effect at start of turn:

  • Heal: Mech restores 100 HP.
  • Augment: Pilot may augment their mech from any available scrap and restore Shield Power.
  • Replenish: Mech restores Skill Points.
